Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about buying, selling and investing in real estate.

You're ready to buy a home when you have stable income, a good credit score (typically 620+), a down payment saved (usually 10-20% of the home price), and you plan to stay in the area for at least 3-5 years. Our team can help assess your financial readiness and connect you with mortgage pre-approval services.

Spring and early summer (March through June) are traditionally the best times to sell, as buyers are more active and homes show better with good weather. However, market conditions, local factors, and your personal timeline are more important than the season. We can help you determine the optimal timing based on current market conditions in your area.

Your offer should be based on comparable sales in the area, the home's condition, current market conditions, and your budget. In competitive markets, you might need to offer at or above asking price. Our agents will provide a detailed comparative market analysis (CMA) to help you make a competitive yet reasonable offer.
